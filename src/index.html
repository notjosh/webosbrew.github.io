Frequently Asked Questions

Is Installing Homebrew Safe?

Yes. Installing homebrew apps and games is safe, and won't void your TV's warranty.
However, although the process of rooting your TV is safe and reversible, you need to be careful after rooting your TV.
See rootmy.tv/warning for more information.

I have XX.YY.ZZ firmware version. Can I root my TV?

Please check the rooting tutorial for the supported firmware versions.
